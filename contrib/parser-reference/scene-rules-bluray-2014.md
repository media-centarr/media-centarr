
# Complete Blu-ray Releasing Standards 2014

> Source: https://scenerules.org/html/2014_BLURAY.html


Effective date: 2014-04-01 GMT. Prior releases exempt unless technical reasons apply.

---

## GENERAL SECTION

**G1**: Release must be exact 1:1 copy of retail disc
**G2**: Group watermarks within Blu-ray forbidden
**G3**: Releases must be region-free
**G4**: New copy protection must be removed
**G5**: Unexplained standard violations require brief NFO explanation

---

## PACKAGING SECTION

**P1**: Sample, NFO, and SFV required per release
**P2**: NFO must include IMDB link, Blu-ray region, audio streams, subtitle streams
**P3**: RAR archives split into 250 or 500 MB segments
**P4**: No passwords or encryption permitted
**P5**: Compression prohibited

---

## TAGGING SECTION

**T1**: Allowed characters are alphanumeric plus period and hyphen
**T2**: Naming templates provided for movies, TV, and music:

Movies: `Movie.Title.YEAR.COMPLETE.BLURAY-GROUP`
TV Series: `Show.Name.SxxExx.COMPLETE.BLURAY-GROUP` or `Show.Name.Sxx.COMPLETE.BLURAY-GROUP`

**T3**: First complete releases tagged without language/region tags; subsequent releases require language or region identifiers
**T4**: Production year required except for TV series
**T5**: Subdirectories labeled DiSC1, DiSC2 (not EXTRA or CD variants)
**T6**: MULTi indicates 2+ audio languages; MULTiSUBS indicates 6+ subtitle languages

---

## PROOF SECTION

**P1**: Physical disc snapshot (JPG/PNG) with group tag required; must be high-quality
**P2**: EXIF metadata must be removed
**P3**: Proofixes accepted within 2 hours of pre

---

## SAMPLE SECTION

**S1**: Approximately 60-second sample cut from M2TS file (not separately encoded)

---

## PROPERS SECTION

**R1**: Allowed only for technical flaws
**R2**: Allowed when previous release stripped menu
**R3**: Directory naming improprieties don't justify propers
**R4**: Different audio/subtitle streams don't constitute dupes

---

## NOTES & SUGGESTIONS

**N1**: Rules effective 2014-04-01 GMT; prior releases exempt unless technical reasons apply
